Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a recipe for scrambled eggs. Something that even someone living in their parents' basement could figure out how to cook.

Disclaimer: I am not liable for this recipe not working for you. I am not a cook.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Water (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs and put in a bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs.
  3. Add water if desired to make eggs fluffier.
  4. Pour eggs into a preheated pan on the stove. Stir and flip with spatula to ensure eggs are fully cooked.
  5. Once eggs look like scrambled eggs, turn off the stove and remove eggs.
  6. Add salt to taste.
