Macaroni & Cheese

Description

A recipe for macaroni & cheese, based on a very heavily modified version of another recipe for baked mac & cheese, in order to work without baking. The first attempt when not baking it was excessively saucy, with not enough cheese, so the following recipe has been modified again. No promises are made that it will taste good.

Original recipe here at Delish Eats Baked Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 12 oz. (3 cups) cheddar, shredded
  • 8 oz. (2 cups) montery jack, shredded
  • 2 oz. (1 cup) Parmesan, finely grated
  • Salt to taste

Potential allergens

  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

Pasta

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the macaroni until al dente, 8-10 minutes. Drain and set aside.

Sauce

  1. Over medium heat melt butter in a large saucepan.
  2. Add flour to the melted butter, cook until blonde, 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add milk, whisking to combine, then raise heat to medium-high. Stir constantly.
  4. Add mustard and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Bring to a simmer and allow the sauce to thicken, about 2 minutes.
  6. Remove pan from heat and whisk in the cheddar, montery jack, and Parmesan, until melted and smooth.
  7. Stir sauce and macaroni together in a pot, and then serve.
