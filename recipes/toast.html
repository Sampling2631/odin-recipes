Toast

Description

A recipe for toast.

Disclaimer: I am not liable for this recipe not working. I am not a cook.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Condiment such as butter

Steps

  1. Get some bread.
  2. Put it in the toaster and turn on the toaster.
  3. Remove from toaster when done.
  4. Apply a condiment such as butter or jelly.
  5. Enjoy.
