PB & J Sandwich

Description

A recipe for a peanut butter & jelly sandwich.

Disclaimer: I am not liable for this recipe not working. I am not a cook.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take 1 slice of bread and spread peanut butter on 1 side.
  2. Take the other slice of bread and spread jelly on 1 side.
  3. Stack the 2 slices of bread, sticky sides facing inwards.
  4. Enjoy your sandwich.
